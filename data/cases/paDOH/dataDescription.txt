#Model: PA DOH

##Raw Data Link: 
https://www.health.pa.gov/topics/Documents/Diseases%20and%20Conditions/COVID-19%20County%20Data/County%20Case%20Counts_6-15-2020.pdf

##Database Description:
This is data direcyly from the PA DOH website. 

##Database Link:
https://www.health.pa.gov/topics/disease/coronavirus/Pages/Cases.aspx

##Contents:
Contains total (up to present) number of comfirmed/probable cases of COVID-19 as well as negative test results and estimatead total tests (calculated by adding comfirmed + negative cases) for each county in Pennsylvania referenced by county name and fips.

##Accuracy:
Most likely good. Straight from the source.

Source: Pennsylvania National Electronic Disease Surveillance System (PA-NEDSS)

##Usefulness:
Limited. Only cumulative data availible but county level testing data could be useful.
